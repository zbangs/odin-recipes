Loco Moco

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 package ground beef
  • 1/4 onion, roughly chopped
  • 1/4 Asian pear, roughly chopped (or apple, pear, kiwi)
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tbsp gochujang
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • High-heat cooking oil

Steps

  1. Blend onion, Asian pear, garlic, soy sauce, mirin, sesame seeds, sesame oil, and brown sugar (until smooth).
  2. Add ground beef to a bowl. Add blended mixture, along with 1/2 of the green onion and gochujang. Mix briefly, until combined.
  3. In a large pan, add a generous amount of high-heat cooking oil. Place over medium heat.
  4. When the oil begins to smoke, add the ground beef mixture and spread across the bottom of the pan. Allow to cook until juices have evaporated and the bottom has become crispy.
